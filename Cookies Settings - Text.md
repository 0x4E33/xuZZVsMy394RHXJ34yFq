## 1. Scope of Application

This policy applies to all personal information you submit while using products and services provided by EPAY, including but not limited to information submitted through the website, mobile application, API, admin console, or other product channels.

## 2. Types of Information We Collect

During your use of the EPAY platform, we may collect the following types of information:

- **Identity information:** such as name, date of birth, ID documents, address, etc. (for KYC/AML compliance)
- **Contact information:** such as phone number, email, etc.
- **Account and transaction information:** such as wallet address, transaction records, on-chain hashes, order numbers, etc.
- **Usage behavior and device information:** such as login IP, browser fingerprint, device type, access logs
- **Risk control and security information:** such as behavioral identification, system risk labels, blacklist matching results

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Account registration, identity verification, payment processing, risk control
- Fulfilling regulatory obligations such as KYC and AML reviews
- Providing service notifications, transaction alerts, security warnings
- System maintenance, data analysis, product improvement
- Partner service integration (e.g., verification providers)

## 4. Use of Cookies

EPAY uses Cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, including:

- Remembering login status
- User behavior analysis and product optimization
- Providing interfaces and recommendations aligned with user preferences

You may disable Cookies through your browser settings, but some features may be limited as a result.

## 5. How We Share and Disclose Your Information

We do not sell user data and only share it in the following scenarios:

- With service providers (e.g., KYC verification, payment gateways)
- To comply with lawful requests from law enforcement or regulatory authorities
- Internal compliance sharing within EPAY affiliated entities

We require third parties to adhere to data confidentiality obligations and sign privacy agreements.

## 6. Data Storage and Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Encrypted storage of user information (e.g., passwords, ID documents)
- Multi-factor authentication and access control
- Cold and hot wallet separation with asset monitoring
- Regular vulnerability scanning and security testing

This platform is not intended for minors under the age of 18. If we discover a minor’s account, we will take immediate action to cancel and delete the associated information.

## 9. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Some of EPAY‘s services are deployed overseas. When cross-border data transfers are involved, we will:

- Comply with relevant laws and regulations
- Sign data protection agreements with overseas recipients to ensure secure transmission
